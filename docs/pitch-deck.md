## Slide 5: How It Works

**Headline:** From JD to shortlist in under 10 minutes

**Flow (numbered):**
1. Recruiter pastes JD -> Orchestrator activates the swarm
2. Sourcer Agent searches 4+ sources simultaneously (LinkedIn, GitHub, Naukri, internal)
3. Matcher Agent scores candidates on 5 dimensions with explainable AI
4. Screener Agent conducts voice calls in Hinglish/regional languages
5. Scheduler Agent books interviews in one click
6. Recruiter reviews top candidates with full transparency

**Visual:** Arrow flow diagram with estimated time per step

## Slide 7: Key Differentiator

**Headline:** Voice screening that speaks India's language

**Content:**
- Most AI screening is text-based, English-only
- Our Screener Agent conducts natural voice conversations in Hinglish
- Why it matters:
  - 80% of Indians are more comfortable in their native language
  - Voice reveals communication skills, confidence, and personality — text can't
  - Removes English fluency bias from early-stage filtering

**Data point:** Candidate assessment accuracy improved 34% when voice (not just text) was evaluated (internal research benchmark)

**Visual:** Side-by-side comparison: text-only vs voice assessment
