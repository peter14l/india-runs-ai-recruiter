# Demo Video Script — AI Recruiter Agent Swarm

> **Format:** 3-4 minute screen recording with voiceover
> **Style:** Walk through the dashboard mockups + architecture diagram
> **Tone:** Confident, clear, India-first

---

## Script

### [0:00-0:20] Hook — The Problem

**Visual:** Dashboard mockup showing 500+ applications, recruiter overwhelmed

**Voiceover:**
"India's hiring is broken. 500 applications per role, 60% of recruiter time spent on screening, and the best candidates — especially from Tier 2 cities, non-English backgrounds, non-traditional paths — get filtered out by keyword matching systems that haven't changed in a decade."

---

### [0:20-0:45] The Solution — Agent Swarm Introduced

**Visual:** Agent swarm diagram fades in (5 agent icons orbiting Orchestrator)

**Voiceover:**
"I built the AI Recruiter Agent Swarm — a system of 5 specialized AI agents coordinated by an Orchestrator. These agents work autonomously: sourcing candidates, ranking them with explainable AI, conducting voice screenings in Hinglish, and scheduling interviews — all in under 10 minutes."

**Show agent chips:** Orchestrator, Sourcer, Matcher, Screener, Scheduler

---

### [0:45-1:30] Walk Through the Dashboard

**Visual:** Screen recording of dashboard mockup

**Voiceover:**
"Here's the recruiter dashboard. You paste a job description, and the swarm activates. You can see real-time progress — Sourcer is searching LinkedIn and GitHub, Matcher is ranking, Screener is conducting voice calls.

Here's the ranked shortlist — 12 candidates above threshold, with scores and explanations for every single one. No black box. You see exactly why Anand is ranked first: his skill match is 38 out of 40, his behavioral signals from open source contributions score 14 of 15."

---

### [1:30-2:15] Key Differentiator — Voice Screening

**Visual:** Assessment report screen

**Voiceover:**
"This is the key differentiator. The Screener Agent conducts voice calls in Hinglish — not English-only, not text-based. It picks up on things a resume never shows. Anand mentioned a microservices migration project that wasn't on his resume. The AI flagged it for human review.

80% of Indians are more comfortable in their native language. Our voice screening removes English fluency bias and actually evaluates communication ability."

---

### [2:15-2:45] Architecture Overview

**Visual:** Architecture diagram with tech stack labels

**Voiceover:**
"Under the hood: CrewAI orchestrates the agents, Gemini 1.5 Pro powers their reasoning, BGE-M3 handles multilingual embeddings for semantic matching, Sarvam AI provides voice in Indian languages, and Qdrant stores candidate and job embeddings for lightning-fast similarity search."

---

### [2:45-3:15] The India Advantage

**Visual:** Map of India / Redrob brand

**Voiceover:**
"This isn't a Silicon Valley idea ported to India. This is built for India's reality — 1.5 million engineers graduating every year, Tier 2 and 3 cities full of untapped talent, and a workforce that communicates in Hinglish, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, and more. Redrob is building India's AI operating system for work. An AI-native hiring layer is the logical foundation."

---

### [3:15-3:45] Impact & Close

**Visual:** Impact metrics slide

**Voiceover:**
"The impact: time to shortlist drops from 5 days to 10 minutes. Recruiter screening time drops from 60% of their day to 10%. Cost per hire drops 70%. And most importantly — no candidate gets rejected without explanation.

I'm a Class 12 graduate building with AI tools. I just experienced the hiring process as a candidate. I know how broken it feels. I'm not here to pitch. I'm here to build what next India runs on."
